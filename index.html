A Simple Process

Here's how we'll work together to bring your site to life.

  • Consultation & Proposal

    We'll spend some time together, in person or virtually, to hone in on exactly what you need. I'll follow up within 24 hours with a project proposal clearly outlining our timeline and milestones, all within your budget.

  • Design & Development

    I'll draft a design based on your goals and preferences, then once approved, development will begin. In addition to our regular communication, you'll recieve weekly status updates until the project is complete.

  • Delivery

    Voila! Your fully customized project will be delivered and ownership will be passed to you. Alternatively, we can discuss plans for continuous development, including maintenance and new features.
